FAQ

Is swimming required?

No, but being comfortable in water helps. Life jackets are provided.

What if it rains?

Trips run in most weather; we may reschedule for safety if river levels are extreme.

Can I bring a camera?

Action cameras with secure mounts are fine. We also offer photo packages.
